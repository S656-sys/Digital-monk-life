🧘‍♂️ Self-Discipline

Train your mind, master your time, and walk your path with focus and faith.

1. Start with Small Habits

Discipline is built in daily actions. Begin with one positive habit like morning journaling or prayer.

2. Set Clear Boundaries

Limit distractions. Schedule screen time. Honor your study, prayer, and work blocks.

3. Embrace Discomfort

Discipline means doing what’s right even when it's hard. Growth lives outside the comfort zone.

4. Reflect & Reset

Track progress weekly. Learn from mistakes. Begin again with grace and grit.
